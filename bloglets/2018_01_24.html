An Interesting Exercise

Daniel Waterworth

January 24, 2018

Try this (and don’t read ahead). You’ll need a piece of paper and a pen. Ready? Do you have a pen in your hand right now? If not, stop reading and get one.

  1. Write down a list of twenty questions. They can be any questions you like but they must be answerable and you must not already know the answer to them,
  2. For each question, write down the effects of knowing the answer and actions you would take,
  3. For each question again, write down either a short list of other questions that would need to be answered first (put these all in another list) or write down what you could do to find out the answer,
  4. Take this new list of questions and go back to step 2. Continue after 2 repeats.

Now, are their steps towards answering those questions that you would like to take? What’s stopping you?
